# Domain Control Runbooks

Synthetic demonstration document for ControlRoom IQ.

## HR and employment

Employment and promotion decisions require human review, role-relevant evidence, fairness checks, and an appeal path.

## Customer support

Customer-impacting denials require current policy evidence. Defective product exceptions may override standard refund windows.

## Procurement

Vendors cannot be approved without compliance documents, due-diligence checks, and risk review.

## IT access

Access removal requires checking leave status, business ownership, operational coverage, and manager confirmation.

## Privacy and data handling

Personal data must be minimised before analytics or model training use. Sensitive complaint details require redaction or aggregation.

## Finance

Payment acceleration requires purchase-order matching and bank-detail verification. Recent bank-detail changes require manual review.

## Security operations

Alert suppression requires checking whether current evidence matches prior false-positive evidence. New suspicious execution patterns must be reviewed.

## Legal and compliance

Liability clauses, data-loss clauses, and audit finding closure require review evidence. Screenshots alone are insufficient for high-risk control closure.

## Operations and safety

Fatigue warnings and other safety constraints must not be overridden for productivity reasons.

## Engineering releases

Payment-path and production changes require integration-test evidence and a rollback plan.
