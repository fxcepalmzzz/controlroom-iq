# Responsible AI Supervision Guide

Synthetic demonstration document for ControlRoom IQ.

## Human approval

High-impact AI recommendations must not be executed automatically. A human supervisor must review the evidence, policy fit, and possible user harm.

## Evidence standards

AI recommendations should include current, relevant, and policy-backed evidence. If evidence is missing, stale, or weak, the supervisor should ask for evidence or pause the workflow.

## Escalation

Escalate decisions involving employment, legal status, financial harm, access removal, safety, or sensitive personal data.

## Refusal and pause

Supervisors should reject or pause recommendations that bypass required approvals, lack citations, or show signs of bias, privacy leakage, or unsafe automation.
