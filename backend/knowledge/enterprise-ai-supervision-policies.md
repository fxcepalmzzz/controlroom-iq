# Enterprise AI Supervision Policies

Synthetic demonstration document for ControlRoom IQ.

## Human review

High-impact AI recommendations must be reviewed by a human supervisor before they affect people, finances, security, access, legal obligations, safety, or customer outcomes.

## Evidence standards

AI recommendations must include current, relevant, and policy-backed evidence. If evidence is missing, stale, incomplete, or not clearly related to the decision, the supervisor should ask for more evidence or pause the workflow.

## Escalation

Escalate decisions involving employment, legal risk, financial harm, access removal, sensitive personal data, workplace safety, or customer-impacting denials.

## Pause automation

Pause automation when an AI system is about to execute an action before required checks are complete.

## Reject

Reject AI recommendations that bypass approvals, lack citations, show signs of bias, misuse sensitive data, or trade safety for speed or cost.
