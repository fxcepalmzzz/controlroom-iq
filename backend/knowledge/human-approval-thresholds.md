# Human Approval Thresholds

Synthetic policy for AI supervisor drills.

## Always escalate

- Employment or promotion decisions
- Account termination
- Vendor approval without compliance evidence
- Customer denial using stale policy
- Recommendations involving sensitive data
- Any action with irreversible impact

## Ask for evidence

Use this when the AI recommendation may be reasonable but the supporting evidence is missing, stale, incomplete, or not clearly relevant.

## Pause automation

Use this when an AI tool is about to execute an action before required checks are complete.
